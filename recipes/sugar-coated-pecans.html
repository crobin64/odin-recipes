Sugar-Coated Pecans

Description

These sugared pecans are wonderful snacks for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 3 quarter teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pound pecan halves

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk together egg white and water in a medium bowl until frothy.
  3. Mix together sugar, salt and cinnamon in a separate bowl.
  4. Add pecans to egg white mixture; stir to coat.
  5. Spread pecans out on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, about 1 hour; stir every 15 minutes.
